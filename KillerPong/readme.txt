KillerPong game

Zombies attacking, closed square, you are trying to kill all 
enemies with paddle and ball. 
Some killed enemies gives some things, like power ups, speed up for moving ball, or speed down (Can be more power ups, like bigger paddle).
There is enemy paddle, who will also try to score points and win. In general you need to try to not miss ball and
try to kill more zombies as possible. After someone misses to hit back, the new ball is given to whom missed to serve.

During some time (90 seconds) you must score the biggest amount of points.

Points count: for killed zombies and when enemy misses to hit back the ball.


This is for the Power Up Table Tennis contest
(http://www.gamedev.net/topic/647849-power-up-table-tennis-contest/)
